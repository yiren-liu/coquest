Title: Some antecedents and effects of trust in virtual communities

Abstract: None

TLDR: Trust was increased through perceived responsive relationships in the virtual community, by a general disposition to trust, and by the belief that others confide personal information.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Computer Science']

Authors: Catherine M. Ridings, D. Gefen, B. Arinze
