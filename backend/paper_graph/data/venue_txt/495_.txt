Title: Sleep patterns and predictors of disturbed sleep in a large population of college students.

Abstract: None

TLDR: It is demonstrated that insufficient sleep and irregular sleep-wake patterns, which have been extensively documented in younger adolescents, are also present at alarming levels in the college student population.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Medicine']
