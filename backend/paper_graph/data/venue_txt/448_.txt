Title: Why do people play on-line games? An extended TAM with social influences and flow experience

Abstract: None

TLDR: This study applies the technology acceptance model (TAM) that incorporates social influences and flow experience as belief-related constructs to predict users' acceptance of on-line games to reveal that social norms, attitude, andflow experience explain about 80% of game playing.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Computer Science']

Authors: Chin-Lung Hsu, Hsi-Peng Lu
