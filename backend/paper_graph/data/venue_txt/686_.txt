Title: Fast Planning Through Planning Graph Analysis

Abstract: None

TLDR: A new approach to planning in STRIPS-like domains based on constructing and analyzing a compact structure the authors call a Planning Graph is introduced, and a new planner, Graphplan, is described that uses this paradigm.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Mathematics']

Authors: A. Blum, M. Furst
