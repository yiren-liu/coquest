Title: The origins and the future of microfluidics

Abstract: None

TLDR: The manipulation of fluids in channels with dimensions of tens of micrometres — microfluidics — has emerged as a distinct new field that has the potential to influence subject areas from chemical synthesis and biological analysis to optics and information technology.

Fields of Study: ['Medicine', 'Environmental Science']
