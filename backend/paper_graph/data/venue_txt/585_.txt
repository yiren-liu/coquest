Title: Hand movements: A window into haptic object recognition

Abstract: None

TLDR: Two experiments establish links between desired knowledge about objects and hand movements during haptic object exploration, and establish that in free exploration, a procedure is generally used to acquire information about an object property, not because it is merely sufficient, butBecause it is optimal or even necessary.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Medicine']

Authors: S. Lederman, R. Klatzky
