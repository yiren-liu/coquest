Title: Statistical Color Models with Application to Skin Detection

Abstract: None

TLDR: This work describes the construction of color models for skin and non-skin classes from a dataset of nearly 1 billion labelled pixels and suggests that color can be a more powerful cue for detecting people in unconstrained imagery than was previously suspected.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: Michael J. Jones, James M. Rehg
