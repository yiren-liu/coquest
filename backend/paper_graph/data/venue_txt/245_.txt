Title: Asymptotic and resampling strategies for assessing and comparing indirect effects in multiple mediator models

Abstract: None

TLDR: An overview of simple and multiple mediation is provided and three approaches that can be used to investigate indirect processes, as well as methods for contrasting two or more mediators within a single model are explored.
