Title: A level set method for structural topology optimization

Abstract: None

TLDR: A new approach to structural topology optimization that represents the structural boundary by a level set model that is embedded in a scalar function of a higher dimension that demonstrates outstanding flexibility of handling topological changes, fidelity of boundary representation and degree of automation.

Fields of Study: ['Mathematics']

Authors: M. Wang, Xiaoming Wang, D. Guo
