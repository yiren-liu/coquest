Title: Differential Privacy

Abstract: None

TLDR: A general impossibility result is given showing that a formalization of Dalenius' goal along the lines of semantic security cannot be achieved, which suggests a new measure, differential privacy, which, intuitively, captures the increased risk to one's privacy incurred by participating in a database.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: C. Dwork
