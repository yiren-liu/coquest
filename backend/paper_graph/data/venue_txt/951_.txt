Title: Automatic Panoramic Image Stitching using Invariant Features

Abstract: None

TLDR: This work forms stitching as a multi-image matching problem, and uses invariant local features to find matches between all of the images, and is insensitive to the ordering, orientation, scale and illumination of the input images.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Mathematics']

Authors: Matthew A. Brown, D. Lowe
