Title: Activity Recognition from User-Annotated Acceleration Data

Abstract: None

TLDR: This is the first work to investigate performance of recognition algorithms with multiple, wire-free accelerometers on 20 activities using datasets annotated by the subjects themselves, and suggests that multiple accelerometers aid in recognition.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: Ling Bao, S. Intille
