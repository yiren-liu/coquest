Title: Who uses Facebook? An investigation into the relationship between the Big Five, shyness, narcissism, loneliness, and Facebook usage

Abstract: None

TLDR: Investigation of how personality influences usage or non-usage of Facebook showed that Facebook users tend to be more extraverted and narcissistic, but less conscientious and socially lonely, than nonusers.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Computer Science']

Authors: Tracii Ryan, Sophia Xenos
