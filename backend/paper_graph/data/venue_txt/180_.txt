Title: Speeded-Up Robust Features (SURF)

Abstract: None

TLDR: A novel scale- and rotation-invariant detector and descriptor, coined SURF (Speeded-Up Robust Features), which approximates or even outperforms previously proposed schemes with respect to repeatability, distinctiveness, and robustness, yet can be computed and compared much faster.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: H. Bay, Andreas Ess, T. Tuytelaars, L. Gool
