Title: Solving the Multiple Instance Problem with Axis-Parallel Rectangles

Abstract: None

TLDR: Three kinds of algorithms that learn axis-parallel rectangles to solve the multiple instance problem are described and compared, giving 89% correct predictions on a musk odor prediction task.

Fields of Study: ['Mathematics', 'Computer Science']

Authors: Thomas G. Dietterich, R. Lathrop, Tomas Lozano-Perez
