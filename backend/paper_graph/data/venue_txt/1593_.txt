Title: Snakes: Active contour models

Abstract: None

TLDR: This work uses snakes for interactive interpretation, in which user-imposed constraint forces guide the snake near features of interest, and uses scale-space continuation to enlarge the capture region surrounding a feature.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: M. Kass, A. Witkin, Demetri Terzopoulos
