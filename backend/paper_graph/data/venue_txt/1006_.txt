Title: Gene Ontology: tool for the unification of biology

Abstract: None

TLDR: The goal of the Gene Ontology Consortium is to produce a dynamic, controlled vocabulary that can be applied to all eukaryotes even as knowledge of gene and protein roles in cells is accumulating and changing.

Fields of Study: ['Medicine', 'Biology']
