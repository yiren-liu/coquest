Title: Parietal lobe contributions to episodic memory retrieval

Abstract: None

TLDR: This article surveys the fMRI literature on PPC activation during remembering, a literature that complements earlier electroencephalography data and proposes three hypotheses concerning how parietal cortex might contribute to memory.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Medicine']
