Title: Protection motivation and deterrence: a framework for security policy compliance in organisations

Abstract: None

TLDR: An Integrated Protection Motivation and Deterrence model of security policy compliance under the umbrella of Taylor-Todd's Decomposed Theory of Planned Behaviour is developed and it is found that employees in the sample underestimate the probability of security breaches.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Business']

Authors: Tejaswini C. Herath, H. Rao
