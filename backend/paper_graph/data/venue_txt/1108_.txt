Title: Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI): Concepts, Taxonomies, Opportunities and Challenges toward Responsible AI

Abstract: None

TLDR: Previous efforts to define explainability in Machine Learning are summarized, establishing a novel definition that covers prior conceptual propositions with a major focus on the audience for which explainability is sought, and a taxonomy of recent contributions related to the explainability of different Machine Learning models are proposed.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: Alejandro Barredo Arrieta, Natalia Díaz Rodríguez, J. Ser, Adrien Bennetot, S. Tabik, A. Barbado, S. García, S. Gil-Lopez, D. Molina, Richard Benjamins, Raja Chatila, Francisco Herrera
