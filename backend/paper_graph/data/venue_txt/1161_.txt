Title: Speed of processing in the human visual system

Abstract: None

TLDR: The visual processing needed to perform this highly demanding task can be achieved in under 150 ms, and ERP analysis revealed a frontal negativity specific to no-go trials that develops roughly 150 ms after stimulus onset.

Fields of Study: ['Medicine', 'Computer Science']

Authors: S. Thorpe, D. Fize, Catherine Marlot
