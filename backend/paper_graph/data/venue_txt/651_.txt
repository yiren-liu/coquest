Title: Silhouettes: a graphical aid to the interpretation and validation of cluster analysis

Abstract: None

TLDR: A new graphical display is proposed for partitioning techniques, where each cluster is represented by a so-called silhouette, which is based on the comparison of its tightness and separation, and provides an evaluation of clustering validity.

Fields of Study: ['Mathematics']

Authors: P. Rousseeuw
