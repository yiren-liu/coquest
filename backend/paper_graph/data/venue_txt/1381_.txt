Title: The Wisdom of Crowds

Abstract: None

TLDR: This work focuses on orthopaedic surgery, where significant variability in site-specific practices of intraoperative neuromonitoring (IONM) for patients undergoing spinal deformity surgery has been uncovered, and the need to dampen “mindless” variation while accelerating “Mindful’ variation and assessing its results.

Fields of Study: ['Medicine']

Authors: Michael Vitale, Kevin Wang, Gregory Pace
