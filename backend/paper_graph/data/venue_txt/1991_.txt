Title: Domain-Adversarial Training of Neural Networks

Abstract: None

TLDR: A new representation learning approach for domain adaptation, in which data at training and test time come from similar but different distributions, which can be achieved in almost any feed-forward model by augmenting it with few standard layers and a new gradient reversal layer.
