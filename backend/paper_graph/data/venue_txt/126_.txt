Title: The relationship between frequency of Facebook use, participation in Facebook activities, and student engagement

Abstract: None

TLDR: Results indicate that Facebook use was significantly negatively predictive of engagement scale score and positively predictive of time spent in co-curricular activities, and some Facebook activities were positively predicting of the dependent variables, while others were negatively predictive.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Computer Science']

Authors: Reynol Junco
