Title: Global physical activity levels: surveillance progress, pitfalls, and prospects

Abstract: None

TLDR: Physical activity levels worldwide with data for adults (15 years or older) from 122 countries and for adolescents (13-15-years-old) from 105 countries are described, with boys are more active than are girls and women are higher in women than in men.
