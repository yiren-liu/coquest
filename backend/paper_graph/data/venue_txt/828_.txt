Title: Factors influencing the usage of websites: the case of a generic portal in The Netherlands

Abstract: None

TLDR: An extension of the Technology Acceptance Model (TAM) to explain the individual acceptance and usage of websites is empirically investigated and a new construct, "perceived visual attractiveness" of the website is introduced and demonstrates that it influences usefulness, enjoyment, and ease-of-use.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Psychology']

Authors: H. Heijden
