Title: A sequential algorithm for training text classifiers

Abstract: None

TLDR: An algorithm for sequential sampling during machine learning of statistical classifiers was developed and tested on a newswire text categorization task and reduced by as much as 500-fold the amount of training data that would have to be manually classified to achieve a given level of effectiveness.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: D. Lewis, W. Gale
