Title: AMC: AutoML for Model Compression and Acceleration on Mobile Devices

Abstract: None

TLDR: This paper proposes AutoML for Model Compression (AMC) which leverages reinforcement learning to efficiently sample the design space and can improve the model compression quality and achieves state-of-the-art model compression results in a fully automated way without any human efforts.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: Yihui He, Ji Lin, Zhijian Liu, Hanrui Wang, Li-Jia Li, Song Han
