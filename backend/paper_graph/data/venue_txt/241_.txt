Title: Data Cube: A Relational Aggregation Operator Generalizing Group-By, Cross-Tab, and Sub-Totals

Abstract: None

TLDR: This paper explains the cube and roll-up operators, shows how they fit in SQL, explains how users can define new aggregatefunctions for cubes, and discusses efficient techniques to compute the cube.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Mathematics']
