Title: Bayesian surprise attracts human attention

Abstract: None

TLDR: A formal Bayesian definition of surprise is proposed to capture subjective aspects of sensory information and it is shown that Bayesian surprise is a strong attractor of human attention, with 72% of all gaze shifts directed towards locations more surprising than the average.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Psychology', 'Medicine']

Authors: L. Itti, P. Baldi
