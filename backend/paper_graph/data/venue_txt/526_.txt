Title: Systematic review of the health benefits of physical activity and fitness in school-aged children and youth

Abstract: None

TLDR: A systematic review of studies examining the relation between physical activity, fitness, and health in school-aged children and youth found that even modest amounts of physical activity can have health benefits in high-risk youngsters (e.g., obese).

Fields of Study: ['Medicine']

Authors: I. Janssen, A. LeBlanc
