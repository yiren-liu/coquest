Title: The Daikon system for dynamic detection of likely invariants

Abstract: None

TLDR: Daikon is an implementation of dynamic detection of likely invariants; that is, the Daikon invariant detector reports likely program invariants, a property that holds at a certain point or points in a program.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']

Authors: Michael D. Ernst, J. Perkins, Philip J. Guo, Stephen McCamant, Carlos Pacheco, Matthew S. Tschantz, Chen Xiao
