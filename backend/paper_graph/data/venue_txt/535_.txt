Title: Dermatologist-level classification of skin cancer with deep neural networks

Abstract: None

TLDR: This work demonstrates an artificial intelligence capable of classifying skin cancer with a level of competence comparable to dermatologists, trained end-to-end from images directly, using only pixels and disease labels as inputs.
