Title: The development of spatial representations of large-scale environments.

Abstract: None

TLDR: The development of the sequence of spatial representations in children conforms to the “main sequence” identified in the construction of spatial representation in adults, as a potential explanation for the extensive parallelisms identified among ontogenesis, microgenesis, the course of pathology, and the recovery of function following pathological insult.
