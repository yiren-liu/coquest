Title: NTRU: A Ring-Based Public Key Cryptosystem

Abstract: None

TLDR: NTRU encryption and decryption use a mixing system suggested by polynomial algebra combined with a clustering principle based on elementary probability theory to create a new public key cryptosystem.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science']
