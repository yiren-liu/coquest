Title: Memory for goals: an activation-based model

Abstract: None

TLDR: The goal-activation model presented here analyzes goal-directed cognition in terms of the general memory constructs of activation and associative priming, and implications for understanding intention superiority, postcompletion error, and effects of task interruption are discussed.
