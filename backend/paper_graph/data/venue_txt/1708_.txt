Title: Global and regional burden of disease and risk factors, 2001: systematic analysis of population health data

Abstract: None

TLDR: Despite uncertainties about mortality and burden of disease estimates, the findings suggest that substantial gains in health have been achieved in most populations, countered by the HIV/AIDS epidemic in Sub-Saharan Africa and setbacks in adult mortality in countries of the former Soviet Union.

Fields of Study: ['Medicine']

Authors: Alan D. Lopez, C. Mathers, M. Ezzati, D. Jamison, C. Murray
