Title: A continuous measure of phasic electrodermal activity

Abstract: None

TLDR: A deconvolution approach is proposed, which separates SC data into continuous signals of tonic and phasic activity, which shows a zero baseline, and overlapping SCRs are represented by predominantly distinct, compact impulses showing an average duration of less than 2 s.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Medicine']

Authors: M. Benedek, C. Kaernbach
