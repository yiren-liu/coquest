Title: Learning the parts of objects by non-negative matrix factorization

Abstract: None

TLDR: An algorithm for non-negative matrix factorization is demonstrated that is able to learn parts of faces and semantic features of text and is in contrast to other methods that learn holistic, not parts-based, representations.

Fields of Study: ['Medicine', 'Physics', 'Computer Science']

Authors: Daniel D. Lee
