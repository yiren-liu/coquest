Title: The Factor Structure of the System Usability Scale

Abstract: None

TLDR: It is recommended that user experience practitioners and researchers treat the SUS as a unidimensional measure of perceived usability, and no longer routinely compute Usability and Learnability subscales.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Computer Science', 'Engineering']
