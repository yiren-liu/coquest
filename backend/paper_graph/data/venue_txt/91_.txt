Title: Convex multi-task feature learning

Abstract: None

TLDR: It is proved that the method for learning sparse representations shared across multiple tasks is equivalent to solving a convex optimization problem for which there is an iterative algorithm which converges to an optimal solution.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Mathematics']

Authors: Andreas Argyriou, T. Evgeniou, M. Pontil
