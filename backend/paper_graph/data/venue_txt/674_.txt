Title: A practical guide to multilevel modeling.

Abstract: None

TLDR: The purpose of this article is to clarify the seven major steps involved in a multilevel analysis: clarifying the research question, choosing the appropriate parameter estimator, assessing the need for MLM, building the level-1 model, building a level-2 model, and likelihood ratio model testing.

Fields of Study: ['Psychology', 'Medicine']

Authors: J. Peugh
