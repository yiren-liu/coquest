Title: A Comparison of Affine Region Detectors

Abstract: None

TLDR: A snapshot of the state of the art in affine covariant region detectors, and compares their performance on a set of test images under varying imaging conditions to establish a reference test set of images and performance software so that future detectors can be evaluated in the same framework.

Fields of Study: ['Computer Science', 'Mathematics']

Authors: K. Mikolajczyk, T. Tuytelaars, C. Schmid, Andrew Zisserman, Jiri Matas, Frederik Schaffalitzky, T. Kadir, L. Gool
