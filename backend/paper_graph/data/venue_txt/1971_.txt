Title: The Expected Value of Control: An Integrative Theory of Anterior Cingulate Cortex Function

Abstract: None

TLDR: This work presents a normative model of EVC that integrates three critical factors: the expected payoff from a controlled process, the amount of control that must be invested to achieve that payoff, and the cost in terms of cognitive effort.

Fields of Study: ['Medicine', 'Psychology']

Authors: A. Shenhav, M. Botvinick, J. Cohen
